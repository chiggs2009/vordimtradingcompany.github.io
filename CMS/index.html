About Us

VORDIM TRADING (PVT) LIMITED, a transportation industry leader founded in 2012, shines as a beacon of reliability and excellence from its headquarters in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe.

Our commitment to trust, excellence, and integrity has made us the leading importer and supplier of premium buses and trucks in Zimbabwe, with enduring client relationships, comprehensive after-sales support, and a dedication to customer satisfaction at VORDIM TRADING.
